Gary Francis Poste Aka Zodiac Killer Caught And Message Code Solved -Explained In Details

A Mysterious Zodiac killer is identified and his name is Gary Francis Poste.

The name Gary Francis Poste shall be remembered now as the most heinous serial killer of all time.

The man who completely terrorized San Francisco in the late-1960s has been identified by the investigators as Gary Francis Poste.

If you are wondering who we are talking about here, it’s the infamous zodiac killer.

He is believed to be the one who is responsible for the 1966 murder case in the SoCal city of Riverside as the team of 40 detectives, journalists, and military intelligence officers came to believe that he was the one to commit murder.

Further, the zodiac case also involves the murder of five people in a span of one year, 1968-1969.

Now, with the killer being publicly known, let’s find out who is he and the details of his crime.

Gary Francis Poste Aka Zodiac Killer Caught And Message Code Solved

The infamous Zodiac killer is identified as Gary Francis Poste but he is not caught.

In fact, he is already dead as he is revealed to have died in 2018.

Poste’s crimes have become a serious case study for the major crimes around the world and no one could ever forget the message code that he sent to the officials.

One can get the preview of the code in David Fincher’s movie ‘Zodiac’ starring Jake Gyllenhaal, Robert Downey Jr., and Mark Ruffalo although the code in the movie may not be real.

He wrote and sent the message code to the newspaper company San Francisco Chronicle and the authorities as well.

Meanwhile, the code is yet to be solved but the letters sent by the zodiac killer are revealed to be deciphered. Continue to know what case breaker has been identified but first of all, let’s find out.

Why Is Gary Francis Poste Named As Zodiac Killer?

Gary Francis Poste was named a zodiac killer after the case breaker broke the case.

The team was reported to have spent several years digging into the new forensic evidence and photographs in Poste’s darkroom.

Adding to their result, they have revealed that they have found a photo featuring a scar on the forehead of the poster.

The same scar is disclosed to be the scar found on the zodiac sketch.

In addition, the team of Case Breaker also deciphered the letter sent by zodiac.

Gary Francis Poste Aka Zodiac Killer Letter Cipher Details

The letter sent by the Zodiac killer to the newspaper agency and authorities was deciphered by Case Breakers who have come to the final decision of identifying Poste as a murderer.

As per the team, there was one particular note where the letters of his full name was removed to reveal another message.

One of the case breakers Army counterintelligence agent have stated that one needs to know the full name of Gary to decipher the anagrams. Further, he believes there is no other way to have figured it out.
