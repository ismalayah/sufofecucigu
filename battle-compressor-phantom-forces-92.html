Battle Compressor Team Flare Gear (Japanese: バトルコンプレッサー [フレア団ギア] Battle Compressor [Team Flare Gear]) is an Item card. It was first released as part of the Phantom Forces expansion.

Card text

Search your deck for up to 3 cards and discard them. Shuffle your deck afterward.
You may play as many Item cards as you like during your turn (before your attack).

Release information

This card was included in the Phantom Forces expansion, first released in the Japanese Phantom Gate expansion. In Japan, a Mirror Holofoil version of this card was reprinted in the Premium Champion Pack; Non Holofoil reprints were later available in the The Best of XY subset, in the Ultra Moon Deck Build Box, in the Extra Regulation Box, and as a S-P Promotional card. The promotional print was one of ten cards available in Extra Battle Day Promo Card Packs, which were distributed at Pokémon Card Gym venues hosting Extra Battle Day tournaments from December 16, 2022. One pack was given to each participant that won a game, while three packs were given to the overall winner of a tournament. One pack was also given to winners of rock paper scissors competitions.[1] The promotional print also features the Extra Battle Day logo in the bottom right corner of the illustration. This card was distributed in the same way for Extra Battle Day on January 1st, 2024, as one of the SV-P Promotional cards.

Origin
