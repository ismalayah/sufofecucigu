Ice Spice Came Into The Spotlight After She Was Seen At A Party With Drake

Ice Spice looks to be on her path to becoming an established rapper in the business. The Bronx native was recently photographed partying with none other than Drake. Spice was spotted having a good time in the audience, while Drake was also seen dancing and singing along to the on-stage performance. Following her arrival in Canada, the 22-year-old posted a video on her social media account in which she came off a private flight wearing a double denim attire with ginger locks. The Neighborhood Talk posted the video on their Instagram page, and the public quickly responded in the comments area.

All About Ice Spice

Ice Spice, born on January 1, 2000, is a 23-year-old singer who rose to prominence after responding to Erica Banks’ song Buss It in 2021. In 2022, she released a track called Bully, followed by another song called Munch (Feeling’ U). Spice had intended to attend a beauty school before pursuing a career in music. Spice has around 280,000 followers on Instagram and approximately 110,000 followers on TikTok, where she provides her musical stuff.

Although specifics regarding her early life are unclear, she was reared in New York City by her mother and grandmother in the Bronx. Her father was an underground rapper, she once said. More information regarding her educational history, financial wealth, and personal life are yet unknown. Ice Spice spent the most of her life inside due to the high crime rate in the Bronx at the time. She ultimately became obsessed to television and YouTube and decided that when she grew up, she wanted to be a renowned celebrity. The artist hopes to perform Latin music in addition to working on some Spanish tracks.

Ice Spice has released a little video for Munch (Feeling U).

On August 15, 2022, Ice Spice released a new video for her song Munch (Feelin’ U). The words of the song describe her feelings regarding dishonest men, disliking women, and other topics. Spice was seen dancing with a group of friends and fans at numerous locations across the Bronx, including a park, in front of Paya Deli, and during a photoshoot. Following its debut, the song garnered a great reception.


Drake, on the other hand, praised Spice’s music in an Instagram DM. According to rumours, Spice will also perform at this year’s OVO Fest with Drake. However, none of the musicians has verified this as of yet. Spice explained to DJ Booth why Bronx hip hop is becoming so famous, claiming that it has occurred since Cardi B placed it on the map for ladies.
