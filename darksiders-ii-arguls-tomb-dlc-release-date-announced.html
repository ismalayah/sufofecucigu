Darksiders II: Arguls Tomb DLC Release Date Announced

Recommended Videos

On Tuesday, September 25th, Darksiders II fans will be able to prolong their experience with the game’s first downloadable add-on, Argul’s Tomb. The pack has a confirmed $7 (560 Microsoft Point) retail price.

The DLC will include:

Two new icy dungeons
New legendary loot
Challenging boss battles

As we previously mentioned, Argul’s Tomb will come with the Wii U version of Darksiders II when it launches on November 18. Conversely, anyone who pre-ordered the game for one of the other three systems, or purchased its Limited Edition, should be able to download the new content for free.

Prolong your time with Death starting next week with the first downloadable content pack for THQ’s action adventure title Darksiders II. The Argul’s Tomb DLC pack launches on Tuesday, Sept. 25 for the Xbox 360, PlayStation 3 and PC. Argul’s Tomb features:

· Two icy new dungeons

· New legendary loot

· Challenging new boss battles

Any new purchaser of the Limited Edition version of Darksiders II will find a unique code in their game box entitling them to download this first content pack for free. Users should follow the instructions on the card to redeem their content. For all other customers Argul’s Tomb is available to download for 560 MS Points / $6.99 / €5.49 / £4.39 / AUD9.25.
