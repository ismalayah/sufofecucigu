Trekking Shoes (Japanese: トレッキングシューズ Trekking Shoes) is an Item card. It was first released as part of the Astral Radiance expansion.

Card text

Look at the top card of your deck. You may put that card into your hand. If you don't, discard that card and draw a card.
You may play any number of Item cards during your turn.

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art Secret card in the Astral Radiance expansion, first released in the Japanese Time Gazer expansion. Both prints feature artwork by Toyste Beach. It was reprinted with different artwork by Amelicart in the special Crown Zenith expansion, first released in the Japanese VSTAR Universe subset. In Japan, the Astral Radiance Regular print was later reprinted in the Ruler of the Black Flame Deck Build Box an in both Starter Deck & Build Sets.

Trivia

Origin

Trekking Shoes are the default style of shoes that Selene wears in Pokémon Sun and Moon. The Trekking Shoes depicted on this card are the same style as Selene's, albeit with a slightly different color scheme.
