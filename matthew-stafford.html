Matthew Stafford Net Worth, Age, Girlfriend, Family, Biography &amp; More

John Matthew Stafford, popularly known as Matthew Stafford is an American who is a popular American football quarterback by profession. He is an American football quarterback with the National Football League’s Los Angeles Rams (NFL). He attended Georgia University and was a first-team All-American.

He was drafted first overall by the Detroit Lions in the 2009 NFL Draft. Stafford is in the top 20 all-time in pass attempts, completions, passing yards, and passing touchdowns. He is third all-time in passing yards per game and was the NFL player to reach 40,000 yards the quickest.

Matthew Stafford Wiki/Biography

Born on 07 February 1988, Matthew Stafford’s age is 34 years as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Tampa, Florida, the United States. He is an American by nationality and has a belief in the Christian religion.

He completed his early schooling at Highland Park High School.

After that, he enrolled himself at the University of Georgia from where he completed his graduation. From the very beginning of his childhood, he was more interested in football and various co-curricular activities rather than in studying.
