WWE Changes Up Johnny Garganos Entrance Theme Song Again During RAW

Fans of Johnny Gargano’s “Rebel Heart” theme song were gutted to hear remix on the November 21, 2022, episode of Monday Night RAW. Tonight, Gargano debuted another remix version of his NXT entrance theme.

Click here for our full coverage of WWE RAW.

Tonight’s edition of WWE RAW featured a singles match-up between Finn Balor and Johnny Gargano. Balor made his entrance first accompanied by other members of the Judgment Day.

Gargano showed up next with Dexter Lumis by his side. The former NXT Champion arrived at the TD Garden in Boston, Massachusetts, to an all-new remix of his Rebel Heart theme song.

Johnny Gargano added on to the Remix theme. I liked it how it was but this aint bad either.

#WWERaw

Gargano has previously asked WWE to restore his original theme song to streaming services. The Rebel Heart made the request following his surprise return to WWE during a previous episode of RAW.
