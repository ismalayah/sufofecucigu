What does Mariah Brown do for a living?

Meri Brown is the first wife of the patriarch of the Brown Family, Kody Winn Brown.

They got married in 1990. Meri was the only sister wife Kody was legally bound to after she became his first spouse.

However, they divorced in 2014 so the former salesperson could legally marry fourth wife Robyn Brown and adopt her three children.

Meri Brown appears to be attaining a comfortable lifestyle after appearing on Sister Wives since its inception in 2010.

However, she earns extra income doing other side businesses.

Aside from being one of the stars on Sister Wives and the earnings that come with it, she runs a bed and breakfast company called Lizzie’s Heritage Inn, which is located in Utah.

On the official website of her bed and breakfast, she also sells merchandise for the Inn.

Per Celebrity Net Worth, Meri has an estimated net worth of $400,000.
