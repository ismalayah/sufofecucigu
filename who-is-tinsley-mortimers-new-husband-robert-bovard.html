Who is Tinsley Mortimers new husband Robert Bovard?

The former star of The Real Housewives of New York City, aged 48, exchanged vows with Robert Bovard in a charming ceremony held in Palm Beach, Florida, this past Saturday, November 11, 2023.

Confirmation of Mortimer’s engagement to Bovard came from a representative last month, following her subtle hints about the impending nuptials in an Instagram Story post. She shared a photo of herself and Bovard with three children, their faces playfully concealed by emojis, captioned with, “Family vacay before the big day!”

Robert Bovard, according to his LinkedIn profile, serves as the president of Augusta Iron & Steel Works Inc., based in Augusta, Georgia, where he has been employed since 2011 in various capacities. His prior marriage to Mary Dial Bovard spanned 12 years until her passing from a brain tumor. Together, they welcomed a son and twin daughters.

Interestingly, Mortimer’s former Real Housewives of New York City colleagues disclosed at the recent BravoCon fan fest in Las Vegas that they did not receive invitations to the wedding.

“I’m not invited,” revealed Luann de Lesseps to the audience, with Kelly Killoren Bensimon chiming in, “I’ve known Tinsley for 25 years and I didn’t know she was engaged,” prompting Dorinda Medley to jest, “So I guess the answer is no.”

This joyous occasion marks Tinsley Mortimer’s second trip down the aisle. She was previously married to Robert Livingston “Topper” Mortimer from 2002 to 2010. Their marriage was a significant chapter in New York’s early-2000s socialite culture, a period that garnered retrospective examination in the 2023 Hulu documentary titled Queenmaker: The Making of an It Girl.
